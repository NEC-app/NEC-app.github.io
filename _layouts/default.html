NEC: Speaker Selective Cancellation via NeuralEnhanced Ultrasound Shadowing

NEC is a light-weight, AI-augment microphone jammer system. Unlike the commercial microphone jammers that jam all involved users and hence being prohibited and unlawful(violation of 47 U.S.C 333) in public usage, our NEC offers a selective speaker jamming service to inhibit microphones from capturing a targetd speaker's voice.

Features

  • How does NEC work?

    NEC uses a trained deep learning model to seperate speaker's voice and generate the shadow wave to cancel/overshadow Bob's voice on attacker's microphone while not impact Alice's communication.
